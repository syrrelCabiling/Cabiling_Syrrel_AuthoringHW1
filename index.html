Welcome to my website! My name is Syrrel and I love everything about art.

A little more about me: I am from the lush country of the Philippines and I moved to Canada when I was 15 years old. I was never interested in any form of art before but I was given a chance to take a Grade 10 Art class; from there I fell in love with sketching, painting, and photography and the rest is history. I also taught myself how to draw and paint digitally which is a bit challenging however I know that I have improved a lot.

Here I showcase to you my favourite photographs that I have taken recently, as well as the video that I did when we went back home in the Philippines almost 2 years ago (I apologize for the shakiness - I was only beginning to explore videography at that time), and a video to welcome 2018. Enjoy!
